Supported Resolutions & Formats

Not all videos have a 4K thumbnail, but we always attempt to fetch the highest quality possible. Here is a breakdown of the image sizes our tool extracts:

  • Max Resolution (maxresdefault): 1280 x 720 pixels. This is the HD version used for high-quality displays.
  • Standard Definition (sddefault): 640 x 480 pixels. Good for blog posts or smaller embeds.
  • High Quality (hqdefault): 480 x 360 pixels. The standard 4:3 image used by YouTube on mobile lists.
  • Medium Quality (mqdefault): 320 x 180 pixels. Useful for thumbnails or preview lists.

By offering multiple resolutions, our downloader ensures you always have the right size for your project. Whether you’re embedding in a blog, creating a presentation, or designing a YouTube SEO strategy, you’ll have the flexibility to choose the perfect format.
