Why Should You Sort Your Hashtags?

A cluttered caption can distract users from your message. By sorting hashtags alphabetically, you create a sense of order. Alternatively, sorting by length allows you to create "pyramid" style blocks of tags that are aesthetically pleasing on platforms like Instagram.

More importantly, using a hashtag manager helps you spot duplicates. Repeating the same tag twice doesn't help your reach—it just wastes character space. Before you post, you can also use our Instagram Hashtag Counter to ensure you haven't exceeded the 30-tag limit.

Key Features of the Hashtag Organizer

  • Alphabetical Sorting: Quickly arrange tags from A to Z or Z to A.
  • Length-Based Sorting: Organize tags from shortest to longest for better visual flow.
  • Duplicate Removal: One-click cleaning to ensure every hashtag is unique.
  • Auto-Prefixing: Automatically adds the "#" symbol if you've pasted a list of plain words.
  • Privacy Guaranteed: All processing happens in your browser; your private tags are never sent to our servers.

How to Use the Hashtag Sorter

  1. Paste: Drop your list of hashtags into the input box. They can be separated by commas, spaces, or new lines.
  2. Select: Choose your preferred sort order (A-Z or Length) from the dropdown menu.
  3. Toggle: Decide if you want to remove duplicates or add the "#" prefix.
  4. Copy: Hit the copy button and you're ready to paste into your social media scheduler or the Caption Cleaner Tool.
