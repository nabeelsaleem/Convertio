Percentage Difference Calculator – Compare Values Effortlessly

Understanding the relationship between two numbers is a common task in finance, science, and everyday life. Our Percentage Difference Calculator is designed to provide a quick and accurate way to compare two positive values. Unlike percentage change, which looks at the growth or decline from an original value, percentage difference treats both numbers equally.

When to Use a Percent Comparison Calculator?

You should use this tool when you have two numbers that represent the same thing, but you want to know how much they differ relative to their average. For example, if you are comparing the prices of two different brands of the same product, or comparing the results of two independent experiments. If you are looking for how much a single value has grown over time, you might find our Percentage of Number Calculator more useful for basic ratios.

How the Calculation Works

The calculator follows a standard mathematical process:

  1. Find the Absolute Difference: Subtract one value from the other and ignore the negative sign.
  2. Calculate the Average: Add the two values together and divide by two.
  3. Divide and Multiply: Divide the absolute difference by the average and multiply by 100 to get the percentage.
