How the Unique List Generator Works

Deduplication might sound complex, but our tool makes it effortless. When you paste your text, the algorithm splits the content based on your chosen separator—be it a new line, a comma, or a space. It then utilizes a "Set" logic to filter out any recurring strings. This is particularly useful for developers cleaning code variables or marketers refining a sorted list of product IDs. You can even toggle case sensitivity to ensure that "Marketing" and "marketing" are treated as the same entry.
