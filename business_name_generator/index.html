Tips for Choosing a Memorable Startup Name

When browsing through the ideas from our company naming tool, keep these professional tips in mind:

  • Keep it Simple: Short names are easier to remember and fit better on logos and business cards.
  • Avoid Trends: Choose a name that will still feel relevant ten years from now.
  • Check Domain Availability: Ensure you can secure a .com or a relevant TLD for your website.
  • Test the Sound: Say the name out loud. Does it roll off the tongue?
