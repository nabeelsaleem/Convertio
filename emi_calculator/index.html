  1. Enter Loan Amount: Use the slider or type in the total amount you wish to borrow.
  2. Set Interest Rate: Input the annual interest rate offered by your bank or lender.
  3. Choose Tenure: Select how long you plan to take to repay the loan (in years or months).
  4. Analyze: Review the Monthly EMI, Total Interest, and Total Payment displayed on the right.

Smart Financial Planning

Before signing any loan agreement, it is wise to calculate the "true cost" of the loan. Often, a lower monthly payment means a longer tenure, which results in much higher total interest. Use this tool to find the "sweet spot" where your monthly payment is affordable but your total interest is minimized.
