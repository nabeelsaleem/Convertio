How to Calculate Robux After Tax for Game Passes

If you are a Roblox developer, you know that the price you set for your Game Pass isn't exactly what ends up in your virtual wallet. Roblox implements a mandatory marketplace fee on all transactions to support the platform's infrastructure. Our Roblox Game Pass Earnings Calculator is designed to help you calculate robux after tax game pass sales with 100% accuracy, ensuring you can plan your development budget effectively.

Understanding the 30% Roblox Marketplace Fee

Roblox takes a flat 30% cut from every Game Pass, Developer Product, and clothing item sold. This means that as a creator, you retain 70% of the revenue. While this might seem steep, it covers hosting, payment processing, and the massive player base Roblox provides. Using a roblox tax calculator is the easiest way to see the "Net Robux" you will receive after a sale is completed.

For example, if you price a "Super Speed" game pass at 500 Robux, Roblox will take 150 Robux as a fee, leaving you with 350 Robux. This 70/30 split is standard across the platform for all user-generated content.

Converting Robux to Real World Currency (USD)

For many developers, Robux isn't just play money—it's a source of income. The Developer Exchange (DevEx) program allows you to convert your earned Robux into US Dollars. As of the current standard, the rate is $3.50 USD for every 1,000 earned Robux. Our tool doesn't just calculate the tax; it also provides an estimated DevEx USD value so you can see the real-world impact of your sales.

Pricing Strategy for Roblox Developers

When deciding how to price your items, you should always work backward from your desired profit. If you need to earn exactly 700 Robux to afford a specific asset or advertisement, you cannot price your item at 700. You must account for the tax. By using our game pass revenue calculator, you can test different price points to find the "sweet spot" that maximizes both sales volume and net profit.

  • Low Tier (10-90 R$): Great for impulse buys and small perks.
  • Mid Tier (100-500 R$): Ideal for significant gameplay advantages.
  • High Tier (1000+ R$): Reserved for "VIP" status or game-changing items.
