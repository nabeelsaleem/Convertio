Understanding Range Syntax

To get the most out of this tool, it is helpful to understand how to input your selection. Our pdf page extractor supports standard range notation:

  • Single Pages: Enter "1, 4, 9" to get just those three pages.
  • Consecutive Ranges: Enter "5-12" to get everything from page 5 to 12.
  • Mixed Selection: Enter "1, 3, 10-15" to combine both methods.

This flexibility ensures you can build exactly the document you need without any manual reordering or complex editing.
