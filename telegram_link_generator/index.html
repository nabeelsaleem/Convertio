How to Create Your Telegram Link

Setting up your direct contact link is straightforward:

  1. Find Your Username: Go to your Telegram settings and copy your username (e.g., @JohnDoe).
  2. Enter Details: Paste the username into our generator. You can also use our username checker if you're looking for inspiration for a new handle.
  3. Add a Message: Optionally, type a message like "I have a question about your services."
  4. Copy & Share: Click copy and paste your new link into your website or bio.
