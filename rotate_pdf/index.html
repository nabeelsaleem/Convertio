How to Rotate PDF Pages Online

Rotating a PDF document shouldn't require expensive software or complex installations. Our Rotate PDF tool provides a seamless, browser-based solution to fix orientation issues in seconds. Whether you have a single page that was scanned sideways or an entire document that is upside down, you can adjust the rotation by 90, 180, or 270 degrees with just a few clicks.

Fixing Upside-Down Scanned Documents

It is common for physical documents to be placed incorrectly in a scanner, resulting in a PDF that is difficult to read. Manually turning your head or rotating your monitor isn't a solution. By using our tool, you can permanently fix upside down PDF files. This ensures that when you share the document with colleagues or clients, it appears correctly on every device. After fixing the orientation, you may want to use our PDF Page Counter to verify the total number of pages in your corrected file.

Rotate Specific Pages vs. Entire Document

Flexibility is key when managing digital assets. Our tool allows you to choose between rotating every page in the file or targeting specific ones. If only page 5 of your report is landscape while the rest are portrait, simply enter "5" in the page range field.
