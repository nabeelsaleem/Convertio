Twitter Thread Formatter – The Ultimate Tweet Splitter

Sharing long-form stories or detailed insights on X (formerly Twitter) can be frustrating due to the 280-character limit. Our Twitter Thread Formatter is designed to solve this exact problem. Instead of manually cutting and pasting your text, our tool acts as a smart tweet splitter, automatically dividing your content into a series of connected posts.

Why Use a Thread Maker?

Threads are the best way to drive engagement on social media. They allow you to bypass character restrictions while keeping your audience hooked. However, manually numbering posts like "1/5" or "2/5" is tedious. Our thread maker handles the math for you, ensuring that even with the added numbering, your text never exceeds the limit.

If you want to add some personality to your threads, you can also use our Emoji Text Generator to make your headers stand out.

Features of Our Long Tweet Splitter

  • Smart Word-Wrapping: We never split words in half. The tool finds the best break points between sentences.
  • Automatic Numbering: Choose between (1/n) or 1. styles to keep your readers oriented.
  • Real-Time Preview: See exactly how many tweets your text will generate as you type.
  • One-Click Copy: Copy individual tweets or the entire formatted thread to your clipboard instantly.
  • Clean Formatting: Use our Caption Cleaner Tool if you need to remove extra symbols before splitting.

How to Create a Twitter Thread

  1. Input: Paste your long text into the input box above.
  2. Customize: Select your preferred numbering style and character limit (280 is standard).
  3. Review: Check the generated cards on the right to ensure the flow of the story is correct.
  4. Publish: Copy each tweet one by one and post them as replies to your initial tweet on X.
