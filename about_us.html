Our Mission

Simplifying your digital workflow with 100% free, private, and powerful browser-based tools.

Who We Are

SimpliConvert was born out of a simple need: to handle common file tasks without the bloat, the cost, or the privacy concerns of traditional software. We believe that tools for merging PDFs, resizing images, or generating QR codes should be accessible to everyone, everywhere, for free.

Privacy First

Unlike other platforms, our tools run 100% in your browser. Your files never touch our servers, ensuring your data stays truly private.

No Barriers

No accounts, no subscriptions, and no registration required. Just open your browser and start converting instantly.

Our Technology

By leveraging modern JavaScript and client-side processing, SimpliConvert provides a fast and secure alternative to traditional file-handling sites. Because all calculations happen on your device, you don't have to wait for uploads or downloads—it's instant and safe.
