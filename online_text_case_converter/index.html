Key Features of Our Case Converter

This tool offers multiple conversion modes to suit every need:

  • Sentence Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence, making it perfect for cleaning up copied text.
  • Lower Case: Converts all characters into small letters, useful for coding or URL slugs.
  • Upper Case: Transforms all text into capital letters for emphasis or headings.
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every word while keeping minor words (like "and" or "the") lowercase.
  • Capitalized Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every single word.
  • Alternating Case: Switches between upper and lower case for a stylized look.
