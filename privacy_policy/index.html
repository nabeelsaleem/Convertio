This policy complies with international standards including:

SimpliConvert is a free online platform offering browser-based tools such as PDF editing, file conversion, and document utilities. Our tools work entirely in the browser and are accessible worldwide without user registration.

We collect two types of data:

a) Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

When you visit our site, we may collect standard usage data including:

  • Browser type and version

  • Device type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • IP address (anonymized in Google Analytics)
  • Referral source (e.g., search engine, direct)
  • Geographic location (country/city-level)

Tools used:

  • Google Analytics
  • Google AdSense

This data helps us understand user behavior, improve the site, and serve more relevant ads.

b) Personal Information (Voluntarily Provided)

We do not require you to sign up, and we do not collect or store personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Uploaded files

If you contact us directly via email (e.g., feedback or support), your email address may be stored solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

3. How Your Files Are Handled

Our tools operate 100% client-side using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your files are processed entirely in your browser.
  • No files are uploaded to our server or third-party services.
  • We do not store, view, or share any documents or data you interact with.

You retain full control of your data while using our tools.

We use collected (non-personal) information to:

We do not sell, lease, or trade user data under any circumstances.
