SimpliConvert ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you access our website and use our tools.

This policy complies with international standards including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and Google AdSense data policies.

1. How Your Files Are Handled (Local Processing)

Our tools operate 100% client-side within your web browser. This means:

  • Your files are processed entirely on your device.
  • No files are uploaded to our servers or third-party cloud services.
  • We do not store, view, share, or have any access to the documents or images you interact with.

Because processing happens locally, your data remains strictly private to you.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require user registration. However, to maintain and improve our website, we automatically collect certain Non-Personal Information when you visit:

  • Browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Pages visited, time spent on the site, and referral sources.
  • IP address (anonymized for analytics purposes).

If you contact us directly via email, we may temporarily store your email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising Cookies

To keep SimpliConvert free for everyone, we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. Please review the following carefully regarding our use of Google AdSense:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • We may use web beacons alongside cookies to collect non-personal data for ad targeting.

Opting Out:

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track anonymized website usage statistics. This service sets cookies on your device to evaluate how visitors use the site. No personally identifiable data is collected or linked to this activity.

For more information on how Google collects and processes data, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data:

  • GDPR (EU/EEA residents): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and port your data. You also have the right to object to data processing and file a complaint with a Data Protection Authority.
  • CCPA (California residents): You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, opt-out of data sharing, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these privacy rights.

Since we do not collect personal accounts or files, our stored data footprint is minimal. However, you can make privacy requests by contacting us at the email provided below.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

SimpliConvert is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or our operational practices. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
