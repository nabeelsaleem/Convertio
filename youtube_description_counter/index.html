The Secret of "Above the Fold" Content

On YouTube, only the first 150 to 200 characters of your description are visible in search results and on mobile devices before the "Show More" link appears. This is prime real estate. If you don't include your main call-to-action (CTA) or a link to your product in this section, most viewers will never see it. Our tool highlights this specific section so you can optimize for maximum click-through rates.

Pro Tips for a High-Ranking Description

To get the most out of your video, start with a 1-2 sentence summary of the video using your target keywords. Next, include a clear link to your website or a lead magnet. After that, use timestamps to help viewers navigate long videos—this also helps your video appear in Google's "Key Moments" search feature.
