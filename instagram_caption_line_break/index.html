Why Do Instagram Captions Lose Line Breaks?

It's a frustrating common problem: you type out a beautiful, easy-to-read caption in your notes app, paste it into Instagram, and when you hit post, it turns into a giant, unreadable wall of text. Instagram's algorithm automatically strips out "empty" whitespace at the end of lines to save data. Our Instagram Caption Line Break Generator solves this by inserting invisible "Zero Width Space" characters or formatting the text specifically so Instagram recognizes the breaks as legitimate content.
