Why Use an Alphabetical Sorter?

Whether you are a student working on a bibliography, a developer organizing code variables, or an office manager arranging a guest list, putting items in alphabetical order is a fundamental task. Manually sorting a long list is not only time-consuming but also prone to human error. Our alphabetize list a-z online tool automates this process, ensuring 100% accuracy in seconds.

Features of Our ABC Sorter

This tool is designed to be more than just a simple sorter. It includes advanced features to handle various text formatting needs:

  • A-Z and Z-A Sorting: Choose between ascending or descending order depending on your project requirements.
  • Duplicate Removal: Clean your data by automatically removing identical entries, similar to our Duplicate Word Remover.
  • Case Sensitivity: Decide whether "apple" should come before "Banana" or if the tool should treat them equally.
  • Multiple Separators: Sort lists separated by new lines, commas, or semicolons effortlessly.

Perfect for Bibliographies and References

Academic writing requires strict adherence to formatting styles like APA, MLA, or Chicago. Most of these styles require the reference list to be in alphabetical order by the author's last name. By using our alphabetical order tool, you can paste your entire reference list and have it perfectly organized instantly. How to Alphabetize Your List

Using the tool is straightforward:

  1. Paste your text into the input area.
  2. Select your preferred separator (usually "New Line" for lists).
  3. Toggle "Remove Duplicates" if you want a unique list.
  4. Click "Copy" to take your perfectly sorted list wherever you need it.
