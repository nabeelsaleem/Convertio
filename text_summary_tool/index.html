Extractive vs. Abstractive Summarization

There are two main types of AI summarization:

  • Extractive Summarization: This method (used by our tool) picks the most important existing sentences from the text. It is highly accurate and ensures that the summary remains true to the original author's wording.
  • Abstractive Summarization: This method generates entirely new sentences to convey the meaning. While more "human-like," it can sometimes introduce errors or "hallucinations."

Our tool prioritizes accuracy, making it an excellent abstract generator for academic purposes where maintaining the original context is vital.

Tips for Getting the Best Summary Results

To get a high-quality TL;DR (Too Long; Didn't Read) result, follow these simple tips:

  • Format Matters: Use bullet points for quick scanning and paragraphs for a more narrative summary.
