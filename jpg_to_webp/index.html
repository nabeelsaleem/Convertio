Why You Should Convert JPG to WebP for Website Speed

In the modern digital landscape, website speed is a critical factor for both user experience and search engine optimization. Large, unoptimized images are often the primary reason for slow-loading pages. By choosing to convert jpg to webp for website speed, you are adopting a next-generation image format developed by Google specifically for the web.

WebP images provide superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. Using our tool, you can maintain the visual integrity of your photographs while slashing the file size. This directly impacts your Core Web Vitals, particularly the Largest Contentful Paint (LCP), which is a key ranking signal for Google.

Benefits of Using WebP Over Traditional Formats

While JPG has been the standard for decades, it lacks the efficiency of modern codecs. WebP supports transparency (like PNG) and high-quality compression (like JPG) but does it better.
