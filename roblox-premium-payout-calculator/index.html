Understanding Roblox Premium Payouts

For modern Roblox developers, revenue isn't just about selling game passes or developer products. The Premium Engagement Payout system allows creators to earn Robux simply by having a high-quality, engaging experience that attracts Roblox Premium subscribers. Our Roblox Premium Payout Calculator is designed to help you forecast these earnings based on your "Premium Playtime Hours" metric found in your Developer Stats.

How to Estimate Roblox Premium Payouts 2026

Roblox uses a proprietary algorithm to determine the exact payout, which means there isn't a single "fixed rate." However, by analyzing community data, we can provide a highly accurate estimate. Most developers see a rate between 0.5 and 1.2 Robux per hour of playtime. If your game has high retention and deep engagement, you might even see rates exceeding 1.5 Robux per hour.

To use this tool effectively, head to your Roblox Creator Dashboard, navigate to "Analytics," and look for the "Premium Playtime" chart. Input those hours into our calculator to see your projected Robux and their real-world USD value.

Converting Robux to USD: The DevEx Factor

Earning Robux is the first step; getting paid in real currency is the second. The Developer Exchange (DevEx) program currently offers a rate of $0.0035 per Robux (or $105.00 USD for every 30,000 Robux). Our calculator automatically applies this conversion so you can see the tangible results of your hard work.

If you are also selling items in your game, you might want to check our Roblox Game Pass Calculator to see how much you keep after the 30% platform tax. Combining engagement payouts with direct sales is the best way to maximize your ROI.

Tips to Increase Your Premium Engagement Revenue

  • Focus on Retention: The longer a Premium member stays in your game, the more you earn. Add daily rewards or long-term progression systems.
  • Premium-Only Benefits: Offer special skins, faster XP gain, or exclusive areas for Premium members to encourage them to choose your game over others.
  • Optimize for Mobile: A large portion of Premium subscribers play on mobile devices. Ensure your UI and performance are top-notch.
  • Track Your Stats: Use our Roblox DevEx Calculator regularly to monitor your progress toward the 30,000 Robux cash-out threshold.
