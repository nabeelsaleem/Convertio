Hashtag Counter – Optimize Your Social Media Reach

In the world of social media marketing, hashtags are the bridges that connect your content to new audiences. However, every platform has its own set of rules. Why Monitoring Your Tag Count Matters

Instagram famously enforces a strict 30-hashtag limit. If you accidentally include 31, Instagram will often strip your entire caption, leaving your post blank and without any searchable tags. By using this tool alongside our Instagram Reel Caption Formatter, you can ensure your posts look professional and function perfectly.

Furthermore, using too many irrelevant tags can lead to "shadowbanning" or reduced reach. This tool allows you to see a clean list of every tag you've included, making it easier to prune the low-performing ones using a hashtag sorter.
