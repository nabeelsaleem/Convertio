Best Practices for Copy-Paste ASCII Art

To ensure your ASCII art looks perfect, remember that it relies on monospaced fonts. These are fonts where every character takes up the exact same amount of horizontal space (like Courier, Consolas, or Fira Code). If you paste your art into a variable-width font environment (like a standard Word document), the characters will shift, and the image will break. Always use a code block or a monospaced setting for the best visual result.
