DevEx Eligibility Requirements

Simply having the Robux in your account does not guarantee a successful conversion. Before using our robux to usd calculator to plan your budget, ensure your account meets the strict criteria outlined by Roblox:

  • Minimum Balance: You must possess a minimum of 30,000 earned Robux in your account. Purchased Robux or Robux acquired through non-developer activities (like trading limiteds) are explicitly excluded from DevEx eligibility.
  • Age Requirement: Developers must be at least 13 years of age. If you are a minor, parental consent and involvement in the DevEx portal setup are required.
  • Account Status: Your Roblox account must be in good standing, with a verified email address, and you must have a registered account with the DevEx payment provider (currently Tipalti).

Gross vs. Net Earnings: Calculating the 30% Marketplace Fee

A common mistake new developers make is confusing their gross item sales with their DevEx eligible balance. It is crucial to remember that Roblox takes a 30% marketplace fee on almost all transactions on the platform (game passes, developer products, and clothing).

If you price a game pass at 1,000 Robux, you only receive 700 Robux in your pending sales. When using our devex converter, you must input your net earned Robux (the 70% you keep), not the gross price the player paid.
