What are the Official LinkedIn Banner Dimensions?

The standard recommended size for a LinkedIn personal profile background is 1584 x 396 pixels. This creates a 4:1 aspect ratio. While LinkedIn can technically accept smaller images, using anything lower than these dimensions often results in pixelation or blurriness, especially on high-resolution monitors.

If you find your image is too large or the wrong shape, you might need to use a social media image resizer to get the proportions exactly right before uploading.

The "Safe Zone" and Profile Picture Overlap

One of the biggest mistakes users make is placing text or logos on the bottom-left of their banner. On desktop, your circular profile picture covers a significant portion of the lower-left corner. On mobile devices, the profile picture shifts toward the center. To ensure your branding is always visible, follow these rules:

  • Keep text to the right: The right side of the banner is the safest place for contact info or slogans.
  • Avoid the bottom edge: Leave a margin at the bottom to account for the profile picture overlap.
  • Check DPI: Use an image DPI checker to ensure your file has enough detail for large screens.

Mobile vs. Desktop: Responsive Design Challenges

LinkedIn uses responsive design, meaning it crops your banner differently depending on the screen size. A banner that looks perfect on a 27-inch monitor might look completely different on an iPhone.
