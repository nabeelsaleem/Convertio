Understanding Aspect Ratios for Social Media

Different platforms require different dimensions. A square 1:1 ratio is standard for Instagram posts, while 9:16 is essential for TikTok and Reels. Using our profile picture cropper presets ensures that your headshot is perfectly centered. By selecting the right ratio before you download, you avoid the awkward auto-cropping that many social media apps perform, giving you full creative control.
