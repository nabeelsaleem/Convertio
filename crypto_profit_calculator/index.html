How to Calculate Crypto Profits and ROI

Calculating crypto profits isn’t complicated once you understand the formula. Our Crypto Profit Calculator uses the following method:

  • Gross Profit: (Sell Price – Buy Price) × Quantity
  • Net Profit: Gross Profit – Exchange Fees
  • ROI: (Net Profit ÷ Total Investment) × 100

This formula ensures you get an accurate percentage return. For example, if you bought 1 BTC at $20,000 and sold it at $25,000, your gross profit is $5,000. After deducting fees, your net profit might be $4,950. The ROI would then be 24.75%. This makes our tool a reliable Bitcoin profit calculator and a versatile crypto ROI calculator.

Understanding the Crypto Exit Strategy Calculator

An exit strategy is crucial for every trader. Our exit strategy calculator for crypto helps you determine the best time to sell by showing break-even points, profit targets, and stop-loss levels. This prevents emotional trading and ensures you stick to your plan.

  • Break-Even Point: The price at which your investment covers costs and fees.
  • Profit Target: Set a percentage gain (e.g., 20%) and see the exact sell price needed.
  • Stop Loss: Plan for downside risk by calculating losses if the market drops 5–10%.

Whether you are swing trading altcoins or holding Bitcoin for years, this tool ensures you have a clear strategy instead of relying on guesswork.
