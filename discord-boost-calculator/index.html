Understanding Discord Server Levels and Boosts

Discord uses a tiered system to reward servers that receive "Boosts" from their members. Each level unlocks specific perks:

  • Level 1 (2 Boosts): Unlocks +50 emoji slots, 128Kbps audio quality, and a custom server invite background.
  • Level 2 (7 Boosts): Unlocks +50 more emoji slots (150 total), 256Kbps audio, 1080p 60fps streams, and a server banner.
  • Level 3 (14 Boosts): The ultimate tier. Unlocks +100 emoji slots (250 total), 384Kbps audio, a custom vanity URL (discord.gg/yourname), and 100MB upload limit for all members.

Nitro vs. Standalone Boosts: Which is Cheaper?

One of the most common questions for server owners is whether they should buy boosts directly or subscribe to Nitro. A standard boost costs $4.99 per month. However, a full Discord Nitro subscription costs $9.99 per month and includes two free boosts plus a 30% discount on all additional boosts ($3.49 each).

If you are trying to reach Level 3 (14 boosts) by yourself, buying 14 boosts without Nitro would cost $69.86/month. With Nitro, you pay for the subscription ($9.99) plus 12 discounted boosts ($41.88), totaling $51.87/month. That is a saving of nearly $18 every single month!
