About Supatool

Supatool was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide powerful, easy-to-use online tools that are completely free. We believe everyone should have access to quality document and image processing without paying expensive software fees.

Today, millions of users worldwide trust our platform for their everyday needs. From students and teachers to businesses and professionals, our tools help people work smarter without complicated software or technical expertise.

All our tools are developed with privacy in mind. Your files never leave your computer - processing happens 100% in your browser. No registration required, no hidden costs, no watermarks.

We're constantly adding new tools based on user feedback. Have a suggestion? Let us know and we might build it for you!
