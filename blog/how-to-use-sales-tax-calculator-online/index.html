Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate sales tax manually? ▶

To do it yourself, you multiply the net price by the tax rate (in decimal form). For example, if the item is $100 and the tax is 8%, you do 100 x 0.08 = $8. But honestly? It's much safer to use a sales tax calculator to avoid simple math errors.

What is the difference between sales tax and use tax? ▶

Sales tax is collected by the seller at the point of purchase. Use tax is what you're supposed to pay yourself if you buy something from out-of-state and didn't pay sales tax. Most people use our free sales tax calculator no login to figure out what they owe in both scenarios.

Does this tool work for international VAT? ▶

Yes! While we call it a sales tax calculator, the math for VAT (Value Added Tax) is the same. Just enter the VAT percentage into the rate field and it will calculate the total just like a sales tax calculator tutorial would show you.

Why is my local tax rate higher than the state rate? ▶

Because cities and counties often add their own "discretionary" taxes on top of the state's base rate. That's why you need the best sales tax calculator 2026—to make sure you're adding up all those different layers correctly.
