What exactly does it mean to remove extra spaces?

Basically, when we talk about this, we aren't just talking about hitting the backspace key. We're talking about "text normalization." This involves three main things:

  • Single Space Normalization: Turning every instance of two, three, or twenty spaces into just one.
  • Trimming: Getting rid of those invisible spaces at the very beginning or the very end of your text block.
  • Line Break Cleaning: Sometimes, extra spaces hide at the end of lines before a hard return. They’re invisible, but they break web layouts.
