What is a Text Sorter anyway?

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Even with a great tool, you can still mess things up. For example, people often forget to remove leading spaces before sorting. If one line has a hidden space at the start, it’ll jump to the top of your "A" list regardless of what the actual letters are.

Another thing? Don't forget that text to emoji letters can sometimes count as multiple characters in terms of length sorting. If you're using the emoji to text generator features, the "length" might not be what you see visually. I learned that the hard way when I was trying to perfectly align a Twitter bio. It was a mess.
