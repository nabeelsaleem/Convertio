What exactly is "Add Line Breaks"?

Basically, it is the process of taking a continuous stream of text and forcing it to stop at specific points. Think of it like putting up fences in a massive field so the sheep don't get lost. You can do this by character count (like every 80 characters) or after every sentence.

When you use a tool to add line breaks, you’re telling the computer: "Hey, after every two sentences, give me a double space." Or maybe: "I need this to fit in a narrow column, so break the line every 40 characters." It sounds simple, but when you’re trying to format a newsletter or a code snippet, it’s everything.
