Frequently Asked Questions

Are meta keywords still important? ‚ñ∂

No. Google has ignored the "meta keywords" tag for over a decade due to spam abuse. Focus your energy on the Title Tag and Meta Description, which directly impact rankings and click-through rates.

What is the ideal length for a meta description? ‚ñ∂

There is no magic number, but Google typically displays up to 920 pixels (roughly 155-160 characters) on desktop and about 120 characters on mobile. Keep important keywords near the front to avoid truncation.

What are Open Graph tags? ‚ñ∂

Open Graph (OG) tags control how your content appears when shared on social media platforms like Facebook, LinkedIn, and Pinterest. Without them, social platforms guess what image and text to show, often getting it wrong.

Why does Google rewrite my meta descriptions? ‚ñ∂

Google rewrites descriptions over 60% of the time to better match the user's specific search query. However, writing a strong meta description is still crucial because it's your default "pitch" and influences Google's understanding of the page.
