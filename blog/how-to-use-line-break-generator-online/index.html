How to use the line break generator properly

It's not rocket science, but there's a specific way to do it so you don't mess up the formatting. I've seen people copy the text and then accidentally delete the invisible characters while trying to add emojis later. Don't do that.

  1. First off, head over to the line break generator.
  2. Type or paste your caption into the box. Go ahead and hit "Enter" as many times as you want. Want a massive gap for dramatic effect? Do it.
  3. Then, click that big "Convert" or "Copy" button. The tool does the heavy lifting by swapping those "empty" spaces for the special invisible ones.
  4. Finally, open your app (Instagram, TikTok, whatever) and paste it. Do not edit the text inside the app after you paste it, or you might break the formatting.
