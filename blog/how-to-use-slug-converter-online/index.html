What is a Slug Converter anyway?

Basically, a slug converter is a tool that transforms text between human-readable formats (like titles) and URL-friendly formats (slugs). Slugs are those lowercase, hyphenated strings you see in your browser’s address bar. They’re vital for SEO because they tell search engines exactly what a page is about without all the messy special characters or spaces that browsers hate.

Between you and me, I used to think I could just write a quick script every time I needed this. But then I’d forget to handle symbols like ampersands or curly quotes, and the whole thing would break. Common Mistakes I've Made (So You Don't Have To)

Early in my career, I thought "the more keywords in the slug, the better." I ended up with URLs that were literally 300 characters long. It was a disaster. Not only did they look like spam, but they also broke my site's layout on smaller screens.

  • Stop words: You don't need "a", "the", or "and" in your slug. "best-slug-converter" is better than "the-best-slug-converter-for-everyone".
  • Double dashes: Sometimes manual editing leaves -- in the middle. A good slug converter for beginners will automatically strip those out.
  • Case sensitivity: Always use lowercase. Some servers are picky, and /My-Page might not load if the user types /my-page.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a slug and a title? ▶

A title is meant for humans to read (e.g., "My Great Blog Post"), while a slug is the part of the URL that identifies the page (e.g., "my-great-blog-post"). Slugs use hyphens and lowercase letters to be URL-friendly.

Is this slug converter really free to use? ▶

Yes! The free slug converter online at SimpliConvert is 100% free with no hidden fees or account requirements. You can convert as many strings as you need.

Does changing my slugs affect my SEO? ▶

Yes, it does. If you change an existing slug, you must set up a 301 redirect from the old URL to the new one. Otherwise, you'll lose your search rankings and end up with 404 errors.

Can I convert slugs back into titles? ▶

Absolutely. Our slug converter guide highlights that the tool works both ways. It can take a hyphenated string and turn it back into a clean, capitalized title automatically.
