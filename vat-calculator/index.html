How to Calculate VAT Inclusive and Exclusive Prices

VAT calculations generally fall into two categories: adding tax to a base price (exclusive) or extracting tax from a total price (inclusive).

  • Adding VAT: If you have a net price and need to find the total, you multiply the net amount by (1 + VAT Rate). For a 20% rate, multiply by 1.20.
  • Removing VAT: To find the net price from a gross total, you divide the total by (1 + VAT Rate). This is often called "reverse VAT" calculation.
