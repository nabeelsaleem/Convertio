Why Calculate Percentage Change in Forex?

While many traders focus on "pips," the percentage change offers a broader perspective on market volatility. For example, a 50-pip move on EUR/USD represents a different percentage shift than a 50-pip move on USD/JPY. By using a forex calculator for percentages, you can normalize your data and compare the strength of different currency pairs more effectively.
