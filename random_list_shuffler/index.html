How the Shuffling Algorithm Works

Our tool employs the Fisher-Yates Shuffle algorithm. This is a mathematically proven method for generating a random permutation of a finite sequence. In simple terms, it's like taking a deck of cards and perfectly mixing them so that every possible order is equally likely. This ensures that our random item picker is as fair as possible.
