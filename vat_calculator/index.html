Add VAT vs. Remove VAT

Understanding the difference between adding and removing VAT is crucial for accurate bookkeeping. When you Add VAT, you are starting with a "Net" price and calculating the total "Gross" price that the customer will pay. Conversely, when you Remove VAT, you are starting with the "Gross" total and working backward to find the original price before tax was applied. This is often necessary when using our Gross Margin Calculator to determine your actual profit after taxes.

To remove VAT, use the formula: Net = Gross / (1 + (VAT Rate / 100)). If the total price is $120 and the VAT is 20%, the calculation is 120 / 1.20 = $100.
