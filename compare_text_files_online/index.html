Why Use an Online Text Diff Checker?

A text diff tool (short for "difference") analyzes two sets of data and highlights the variations. Our tool is designed for simplicity and speed. Instead of opening complex software, you can simply paste your text and get a side-by-side comparison in seconds.

  • Visual Highlighting: Instantly see additions in green and deletions in red.
  • Side-by-Side View: Compare the original and modified versions line-by-line.
  • Privacy First: Your text is processed entirely in your browser. We never store or see your data.
  • Zero Installation: No need to download heavy software like WinMerge or DiffMerge.

How to Use the Text Comparison Tool

Using our tool is straightforward. Follow these simple steps to compare your documents:

  1. Paste your original text into the left-hand box labeled "Original Text".
  2. Paste the updated or modified version into the right-hand box labeled "Changed Text".
  3. Click the "Compare Now" button.
  4. Review the results below, where differences are clearly highlighted.
