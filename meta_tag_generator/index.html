Why Meta Tags Still Matter in 2025

I've been doing SEO for over a decade, and I keep hearing that meta tags are dead. They’re not. While Google doesn’t use meta keywords anymore (haven’t since 2009), your title tag and meta description are still your billboard on the search results page. I’ve seen clients double their click-through rates just by rewriting these tags. Tips for Best Results

1. Front-load your keywords: Put your primary keyword near the beginning of your title tag.
2. Write for humans: Your meta description should read like a compelling ad, not a list of keywords.
3. Keep it unique: Every page deserves its own tags. Duplicate tags confuse search engines and users.
4. Test on mobile: Most searches happen on phones. Our mobile preview shows you exactly what mobile users see.
5. Update quarterly: CTR dropping? Refresh your meta description. I do this for my top 20 pages every three months.
