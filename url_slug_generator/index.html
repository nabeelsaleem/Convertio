Why Use a URL Slug Generator?

A URL slug is the specific part of a web address that identifies a page in a human-readable format. Instead of having a URL like example.com/p=123, a slug allows you to have example.com/how-to-generate-slugs. SEO Best Practices for URL Slugs

Search engines like Google use the URL to understand what a page is about. To maximize your SEO potential, follow these rules:

  • Keep it Short: Aim for 3-5 words that describe the core topic.
  • Use Hyphens: Search engines prefer hyphens over underscores as word separators.
  • Include Keywords: Ensure your primary target keyword is present in the slug.
  • Avoid Dates: Unless the content is time-sensitive, avoid putting years or months in the slug to keep it evergreen.
  • Lowercase Only: Stick to lowercase letters to avoid 404 errors on case-sensitive servers.
