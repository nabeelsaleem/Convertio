What is Aspect Ratio and Why Does It Matter?

Aspect ratio is the proportional relationship between the width and height of an image or screen. For example, a 16:9 ratio means that for every 16 units of width, there are 9 units of height. This is the standard for modern televisions and YouTube videos. Using a 16:9 calculator ensures that when you resize a 4K video to 1080p or 720p, the image remains crisp and correctly framed.
