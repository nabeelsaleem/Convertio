Why You Need to Generate Dummy Text for Web Design

The History of Lorem Ipsum

Lorem Ipsum has been the industry's standard dummy text since the 1500s. It originated from a scrambled section of Cicero's "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum," a Latin philosophical work.
