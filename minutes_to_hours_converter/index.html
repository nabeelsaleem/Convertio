Why Convert Minutes to Decimal Hours?

In the professional world, especially when using a Freelance Hourly Rate Calculator, time is often represented as a decimal. For instance, 90 minutes is 1.5 hours. Decimal representation makes it significantly easier to multiply time by an hourly rate to determine total earnings or costs. This tool eliminates the manual math and potential for error, ensuring your invoices and time logs are always accurate.
