How to Choose the Perfect Character Name

When using a character creator tool, consider the "vibe" of your story. A character named "Balthazar Stormborn" fits perfectly in a high-fantasy epic but would feel out of place in a modern legal thriller. Here are a few tips for selecting the right name:

  • Consider the Era: Use our "Vintage" filter for historical fiction to avoid anachronistic names.
  • Check for Alliteration: Names like "Peter Parker" or "Severus Snape" are memorable because of their rhythm.
  • Avoid Similar Sounds: If your protagonist is "John," don't name the antagonist "Jonah." It confuses the reader.
  • Use Meaning: Many writers use our baby name generator mode to find names with specific etymological meanings that reflect a character's destiny.
